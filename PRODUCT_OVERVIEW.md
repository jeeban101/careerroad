**CareerRoad** is an AI-powered career development platform designed specifically for Gen Z students and early graduates who are confused about their career paths. We provide personalized, step-by-step roadmaps that bridge the gap between current education and dream career roles.

## üí° The Problem We Solve

### Target Audience Pain Points:

- **Skills Gap**: Unclear about what skills are needed for specific roles
- **Lack of Direction**: No structured guidance from education to employment
- **Information Overload**: Too many career options without clear pathways
- **Experience Gap**: Don't know how to gain relevant experience

### Market Need:
- 70% of Gen Z students feel uncertain about their career direction
- Traditional career counseling is expensive and not scalable
- Generic career advice doesn't account for individual backgrounds
- Need for personalized, actionable career guidance
